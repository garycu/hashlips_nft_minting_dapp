SkywalkerZ is an impact NFT collection whose proceeds fund the work of Dream DAO, a global community powered by Civics Unplugged which empowers diverse, regen-minded Gen Zers to be and build the future of web3 x social impact. The second batch of SkywalkerZ is minting in June 2022.

Launched January 2022, Dream DAO partners with organizations like Gitcoin, Celo, Coinbase, and more to provide training, mentorship, funding, and internship opportunities Gen Zers need to onboard to and contribute meaningfully to regen web3.

Purchasers of a SkywalkerZ NFT can apply to become "Champions" of the Dream DAO, who mentor the Gen Zers in our community and shape the future of the DAO. Season 2 of the Dream DAO beings July 1st, 2022.
